Are Precious Metals Right For You?

Precious metals provide a distinctive safeguard against inflation and dedollarization. Consider this: if other investment or savings avenues falter, gold and silver are securely stored in a safe or depository, remaining within your reach. Unlike currency, gold or silver cannot be printed by the government. They serve as a form of insurance against financial turmoil or political/military disturbances. In scenarios where conventional investment choices face challenges or collapse, precious metals operate independently from the digital financial realm. This isolation from upheaval renders them the ultimate safe-haven asset.
Gold
Unlike the US dollar, which has lost 98% of its purchasing power since 1971, gold’s purchasing power remains stable over time. It has proven its worth in the face of inflation, recession, currency fluctuation, market collapse and social upheaval.
